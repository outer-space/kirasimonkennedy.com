Kira Simon-Kennedy

I help creative people do impactful & interesting things as the co-founder & director of China Residencies, a co-founder of Rivet, and an independent film producer.

I like writing guides, mentoring collectives, and curating meme tactics.

These days I mostly go between New York, and the 50+ places we work with in mainland China & Hong Kong.

Currently

  • China Residencies

    nonprofit founded in 2013 to foster sustainable creative exchange by creating a free online directory of arts opportunities in mainland China & Hong Kong, funding excellent creative people and projects, and nurturing the next generation of organizers and administrators.

  • The Untitled P.R.C. Project

    feature documentary directed by Jessica Kingdon on the changing nature of labor, production, and aspiration in the People's Republic of China

  • Rivet

    unified search platform for creative opportunities worldwide
