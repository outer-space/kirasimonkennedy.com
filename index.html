Kira Simon-Kennedy

I help creative people do impactful and interesting things as the co-founder & co-director of 爻 yáo collaborative, and an independent film producer.

I like writing guides, redistributing resources, and curating meme tactics.

These days I mostly live in Paris, studying "Transformations & Transitions Socio-Écologiques," urban planning, biodiversity & ecology at the Jardins des Plantes & Paris 8. I have mixed feelings about twitter and instagram, but I'm always happy to mentor justice-oriented nice people with projects for the public good ~ the best way to get in touch if you want to work together is to email sk.kira {at} gmail.

Currently

  • 爻 yáo collaborative | co-founder & co-director

    爻 yáo collaborative (previously China Residencies) maps and weaves the local and global constellations of artists, collectives, culture workers and stewards whose work opens portals for cross-border artistic exchange, friendship, and care between mainland China, Hong Kong and the rest of the world.

  • Film projects in progress | producer

    I'm currently producing first feature documentaries by artists Jiayu Yang (The Entomologists); Shelley Cheung (Laying The Last Track); AX Mina (Rubbish); Yeelen Cohen (Lights of Passage), Morehshin Allahyari (The Remaining Signs of Future Centuries) and Mia Bendrimia (Magma). I'm also a board member of the Documentary Producers Alliance. Et si vous êtes à Paris, rejoignez nous au ciné-club Tonnerre :)
